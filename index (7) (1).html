Ultrasound TAT Trend
TAT Distribution by Process – Ultrasound
  • Daily Ultrasound completed volume vs average TAT with target reference.
  • Ultrasound TAT shows significant day-to-day variability and frequently exceeds target, with the highest TAT exceeding 3 hours.
  • Wait time is the dominant contributor to service TAT (longest 05:06, median 00:48), driven primarily by arrival surges and patient readiness before service rather than scan duration.
  • Strengthening patient preparation and readiness workflows prior to service would reduce queue buildup (wait time) and improve overall Ultrasound TAT performance.
